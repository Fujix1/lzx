MDX conversion preserves the clear title and PDX-name header, then replaces the compressed body with decoded MDX data. PDX conversion is currently a whole-file LZX042 attempt until more compressed PDX samples are verified.

デコーダ処理: Mamiya's GPL lzx042.nas from the KUMAamp project, as mirrored at FIX94/in_mdx.
